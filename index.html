1. PAIN (NAGATO UZUMAKI)

the Village Hidden in the Rain

Nagato (known better as Pain) was the recognized leader of Akatsuki and a God of Amegakure. Reffered to as Leader by all akatsuki members, except for Konan, he always directed the actions of the rest of the group and maintained authority over them. However, he had secretly collaborated with Uchiha Madara. According to Madara, Nagato is a descendant of the Uzumaki clan. During the Second Great Shinobi World War, Nagato lived with his parents in a village near Amegakure. One day two Konoha shinobis broke into their home looking for food, unaware that the place was still inhabited. Nagato's parents, believing they were going to killed, attacked the two so that Nagato could scape. Having mistaken them to be enemy ninjas, the konoha ninjas killed his parents. In his grief, Nagato awakened his Rinnegan and killed the assailants. He would come to consider this the First Great Pain Of His Life.

2. KONAN

The Village Hidden in the Rain

Konan was a kunoichi belonging to the Village Hidden by the Rain. She was one of the pupils of Jiraiya, one of the Three Legendary Sannin of the Hidden Leaf Village. Konan played a significant role in the series. She was always by Nagato Uzumaki’s side, helping him in his goals, and played a major role and shaping the story of the series. Konan was one of the three founding members of the popular organization, Akatsuki. She founded the Akatsuki along with Nagato and Yahiko and became its leader. Akatsuki was founded with the goal to end the era of war and establish peace in the war-inflicted they were living in. Hanzo of the Salamander, the leader of the Hidden Rain Village at the time, was threatened by Akatsuki’s growing influence in the Hidden Rain Village and feared Yahiko, the leader of the Akatsuki, might overthrow him to become the leader of the Hidden Rain Village.

3. ITACHI UCHIHA

The Village Hidden in the Leaves

Itachi is a complex character in the series. Itachi Uchiha was a rogue shinobi from the Village Hidden in the Leaves. He was a member of the legendary Uchiha Clan and even among the Uchiha, he possessed great intellect and skills. He was a prodigy of the Uchiha clan and showcased his superior skills at a young age. He was the youngest member of Anbu Black Ops. He later fled the Hidden Leaf Village after he murdered all his clan members, gaining him the nickname Clan Killer Itachi. This incident later became known as the Uchiha Clan Massacre and The Downfall of the Uchiha Clan. He murdered his clan member and his parents, except his younger brother, Sasuke Uchiha. As a response to this development, Itachi became a rogue ninja of the Hidden Leaf and became one of the most wanted criminals in the Bingo Book. After the massacre, Itachi joined the Akatsuki, a criminal organization consisting of strong rogue shinobi from different Hidden Villages.

4. KISAME HOSHIGAKI

The Village Hidden in the Mist

Kisame Hoshigaki is also known as the Monster of the Hidden Mist. He is from Kirigakure and belongs to the Hishigaki clan. After he joined the Seven Swordsmen of the Mist, he became an S-rank missing-ninja. He, later on, joined the Akatsuki and was partnered up with Itachi Uchiha. When Kisame was part of the Seven Swordsmen of the Mist, he was a guard for the Cypher Division. When they were attacked by Konoha shinobis under Ibiki, Kisame killed everyone in the Cypher Division so that the intel cannot be obtained by Konoha. When he got to know that his senior was leaking information to the other villages, Kisame killed him and took his sword Samehada for himself. Even though he was praised for his loyalty to Kiri, Kisame came under the impression that the world is only filled with liars.

6. HIDAN

The Village Hidden in Hot Water

Hidan belonged to Yugakure, also known as the Village Hidden in Hot Water, and was an S-rank shinobi. Unfortunately, very little is known about his childhood as his past is still shrouded in mystery. Hidan grew enraged with how things were progressing after Yugakure turned from a shinobi village to a tourist destination. Before fleeing Yugakure and joining the cult-like Jashin religion, he killed his neighbors because he believed the shinobi who lived there were meant to be killed. The way of Jashin worships a deity named Lord Jashin and its core principle central tenet appears to be a blatant slaughter, with anything short of complete annihilation being condemned as sin. Hidan was granted immortality after testing Jashin's undiscovered method. After joining the Jashinists, he started believing that anyone who does not worship Jashin is meant to be killed.

7. KAKUZU

The Village Hidden in the Waterfall

Kakuzu belonged to Takigakure, also known as the Village Hidden in the Waterfall, and was an S-rank shinobi. Unfortunately, very little is known about his childhood and past as his heritage is still shrouded in mystery. Because of his formidable abilities, the higher-ups of Takigakure assigned him a mission to assassinate The First Hokage of Konoha, Hashirama Senju, who is renowned as the God of Shinobi. Although he was one of the strongest shinobi of his time, he was still no match for Hashirama Senju. Despite the fact that he laid his life on the line for his village and faced overwhelming danger, he was severely punished for failing in his mission. Kakuzu developed a fierce hatred for Takigakure as he escaped from prison as a result of this treatment. Kakuzu killed the village elders, stole their hearts, and left with the knowledge of the most valuable kinjutsu (forbidden jutsu) in the village, known as Earth Grudge Fear. He earned a living as a rogue ninja by engaging in black market bounty hunting and ripping out the still-beating hearts of formidable shinobi in order to lengthen his own lifespan and prowess.

8. SASORI

The Village Hidden in the Sand

Sasori was a member of the infamous criminal organization, the Akatsuki, and was a rouge shinobi from the Village Hidden in the Sand. Sasori played the role of an antagonist in the series. In the anime, Sasori was an innocent child and was traumatized by his parent’s unfortunate demise and grew colder as he grew. Sasori was a master puppeteer and designed most of the puppets available in the Hidden Sand’s arsenal. He was renowned as Sasori of the Red Sand. He was a member of the Puppet Brigade of the Hidden Sand Village. As he grew older, his obsession for puppets grew and he started creating Human Puppets of strong individuals. Human Puppets were unique puppets that were created by the corpse of an individual. Human Puppets were special and surpassed normal puppets by many folds as they contained the chakra and jutsu the shinobi possessed when they were alive.

9. ZETSU

Otsutsuki Reincarnation

Black Zetsu started conflicts and sowed discord between Indra, Ashura Otsutsuki, and each of their reincarnations. He utilized Madara Uchiha, who was the Ten-Tails Jinchuriki at the moment, as a tool to resurrect his mother Kaguya Otsutsuki, and betrayed him. Black Zetsu was the only person in the Naruto world with the remarkable talent known as Body Coating Technique. He could use the plasticity of his own body to cover the target with this technique. He was able to use all of the methods and Kekkei Genkai that the target had by using Body Coating. He employed Rinne Rebirth Jutsu to revive Madara by performing this technique on Obito Uchiha. He was the villainous counterpart of his brother, Hagoromo Otsutsuki, the Sage of Six Paths. He was Hagoromo Otsutsuki's evil twin and the Sage of the Six Paths, Hagoromo Otsutsuki. He appeared to be black, although Hagoromo had a white complexion. In contrast to Hagoromo, who rebelled against their mother, he was the dutiful child. Black Zetsu revived their mother after Hagoromo had sealed her away. They flawlessly reflected each other's behavior and intentions.

10. TOBI

The Village Hidden in the Leaves

Obito Uchiha was a formidable shinobi originally belonging to the Village Hidden in the Leaves. He was a member of the formidable and legendary Uchiha Clan, feared across nations for their visual prowess. Obito was one of the strongest shinobi the Uchiha clan or the shinobi world ever witnessed in their long-lasting history. Obito was born and grew up in the Hidden Leaf Village and eventually became a member of a genin squad consisting of Kakashi Hatake, Rin Nohara, and himself led by Minato Namikaze. Although his abilities may have faded in comparison to Kakashi in his early years as a shinobi, he surpassed his predecessors on many levels during his prime. Obito was believed to have died in the Third Great Ninja War but he was rescued by Madara Uchiha, also known as the Ghost of the Uchiha. After he witnessed the death of Rin, his love interest, he joined forces with Madara and soon took his identity to realize their dream of a peaceful world by using the Infinite Tsukuyomi.
